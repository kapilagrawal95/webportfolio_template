Interactive Landslide Simulator: A Game for Landslide Risk Awareness

We created a web-based decision-making game for improving the landslide risk awareness among people of Himalayas in Northern India. Presently, we are applying reinforcement-learning models like Expectancy Valence (EV) and Prospect Valence (PV) models to explain the decision-making abilities of humans. Understanding Navigational Behavior of Uber Drivers

We were interested to know how uber drivers should navigate in a city to maximize their daily earnings. For this purpose, we created an Artificial Environment using pygame. Navigational Behaviour were broadly classified into three groups: Greedy Walk, Random Walk and Sedentary Walk and for each setting average earning over 3 hours were plotted.
