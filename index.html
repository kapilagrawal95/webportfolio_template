Kapil Agrawal

My name is Kapil and I am a recent graduate from Delhi Technological University. I am currently working with Dr. Varun Dutt as a Project Associate at Indian Institute of Technology at Mandi, Himachal Pradesh. Here, I delve in understanding and demystifying several factors that trigger a landslide and make generative machine-learning models to predict them well before they turn hazardous. On a related note, I also work on developing reinforcement learning models to understand the risk perception of people living in Landslide prone areas. I have been able to achieve some good results from my work and have published them. A more detailed discussion can be found in the Publications page.

My interests include:

  • Artificial intelligence
  • Probabilistic Programming
  • Decision theory
  • IoT
  • Cognitive Modeling
